THE ANSEM PROMISE

Creator fees that build the floor.

When a token tied to Ansem ran to a nine-figure market cap in days, it breathed life back into the Solana trenches. What carried further than the chart was Ansem’s idea — that a creator could route their fees straight into a token’s liquidity instead of taking them off the table. We named that idea the Ansem Promise, built it into $ANSTRACK, and made every injection verifiable on-chain.

50% of all creator fees are routed directly into the $ANSTRACK liquidity pool — no discretionary payouts, no “trust us.” The remaining 50% funds project advancement: DEX updates, listings, and building out the ANSTRACK system.

Because it settles on Solana, every injection leaves a signature you can open in an explorer and check yourself.
